Help & FAQs

Find answers to common questions about Tech4Hope courses, application process, fees, and more. If you can't find what you're looking for, please contact our support team.

How do I apply for a course?

You can apply for any available course directly from your dashboard by navigating to the "Apply for Course" section. Browse the list of courses, read their descriptions, and click "Apply Now" for your desired program. Our team will then reach out to you for further steps.

What are the eligibility criteria for courses?

Eligibility criteria vary by course. Generally, candidates should have completed their 10th grade. Some advanced technical courses might require specific prior knowledge or qualifications. Please check the individual course descriptions on the "Apply for Course" page or contact us for details.

How can I check my fee details and make payments?

Your dashboard provides a "Fee Details" section where you can view your total course fees, paid amounts, and outstanding dues. Currently, payments are handled offline at our centers. Please visit your nearest Tech4Hope center or contact administration for payment instructions.

How is attendance recorded?

Attendance is recorded daily by your instructors at the training center. You can view your attendance records and overall attendance rate in the "Attendance Data" section of your dashboard. Regular attendance is mandatory for course completion and certification.

Can I change my course after applying?

Course changes are subject to availability and administrative approval. Please contact your center's administration as soon as possible if you wish to change your enrolled course. Terms and conditions may apply.
